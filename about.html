What is CMU Pickleball?

Hey pickles,

Welcome to CMU Pickleball! While we began not too long ago in Spring 2023, we are already a fully-formed club filled with excellent culture and tons of fun.

We host tons of events, including weekly open play, hosting tournaments, travelling to tournaments and colleges, and much more.

If you want to see what CMU Pickleball is all about, please check out the Links Tab, which links to our social medias. We heavily document club activities, and we try to post everything to our YouTube and Instagram accounts.

If you want to learn pickleball, play in casual or competitive environments, or are generally a fan of racket sports, look no further than CMU Pickleball!

Dink ya later,
