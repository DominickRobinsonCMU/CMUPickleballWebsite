Pickleball Club - The Dawn of Pete the Pickle

"In the quaint town of Dinklesburgh, "Pickleball Club: The Dawn of Pete the Pickle" follows the riveting journey of Pete the Pickle, who emerges from a life of simple pleasures into the thrilling and competitive world of pickleball. With each swing of the paddle, Pete discovers the depth of his determination and the strength of his spirit, navigating the complexities of youth, education, and friendship. His passion for the sport and the drive to excel propel him into a world where the stakes are much higher than he ever imagined.


Pete's journey is not just about sportsmanship but about the bonds that form on the journey to greatness. As rivalries intensify and challenges mount, Pete finds himself at the heart of a saga that tests the limits of his courage and the power of his friendships. This tale is a testament to the enduring spirit of competition and the unbreakable bonds that pickleball fosters among those who play it. Without revealing the twists and turns of Pete's adventure, this story is a reminder that the true essence of victory lies in the journey itself and the friends we make along the way."


While the story of this game serves as the origin story for the {ete the Pickle, the mascot of CMU Pickleball, it is also loosely based on the history of the club istelf. It is a love letter to the club, and the vast majority of the characters who appear in game are references to real-life club members. This game was developed in tandem with a real-life booth shown off at CMU's 2024 Carnival members. We all hope that you sincerely enjoy the game!
